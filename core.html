Core

GOAL

Weight loss ,Muscle toning ,Sport fitness

MODALITY

Endurance

INTENSITY

3

Core employs Suspension Training, Slam Balls, and Core Bags to develop your core muscles. Using a rope system, you can exercise against your own body weight while adjusting the angle for changing resistance. Slam Balls and Core Bags provide variation while challenging different muscle groups. The lesson is appropriate for all fitness levels, with configurable intensity via weights and body angles. Core is a versatile and complete workout for core strength and stability. Core.
