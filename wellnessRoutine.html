Weekend Wellness Routine


Why need wellness routine in our life?

Eat Better:

Since fast food and snacks are frequently convenient options, it is simpler to have an unhealthy diet when there isn't a set time for grocery shopping. Making time to schedule your grocery store visits allows you to consider what wholesome meals and foods you may include in your daily routine.

Get Motivation:

Scheduling regular exercise is easier when you give it time. You may manage your time more effectively by scheduling your workouts and maintaining an active lifestyle with a daily regimen.

Improve your ability to manage stress:

Stress can have a detrimental effect on your general health and increase your risk of heart disease. An efficient regimen can aid in lowering stress, which can improve mental health and result in more leisure time, reduced anxiety, and more downtime.

Improve your sleep:

Routines such as your sleep schedule and bedtime routine have an impact on your energy, emotional stability, and mental clarity. You can sleep better if you keep your bedtime and wake-up times regular.

Below are FYI weekend wellness routines, and if you haven't developed your own personal wellness routine yet, this would be a good time to quickly develop your own personal schedule.

A Wellness Routine Example:

Time Activity
6:00 a.m. Wake up & stretch your body
7:00 a.m. Drink hot water or coffee to refresh your mind
8:00 a.m. Do some exercise to maintain a healthy routine
9:00 a.m. Start the day with breakfast - fruits, protein in appropriate quantity
1:00 p.m. Have a rest and take a healthy lunch - such as salad
3:30 p.m. Healthy snacks - such as dried fruits, nuts
6:00 p.m. Outdoor exercise followed by a healthy dinner - less protein
10:30 p.m. Stretch again before sleep, end the day with a healthy routine!
